Golden milk tea

Description

This golden milk tea is perfect to relax your body whenever you're ready to go to bed.

Easy to prepare, fragrant and sweet, it is sure to bring you the sweetest of dreams.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons of honey or syrup

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a teapot.
  2. Heat on low and steep for 3 to 5 minutes.

Definitely the simplest and shortest recipe yet! Enjoy your tea and sleep soundly :3
